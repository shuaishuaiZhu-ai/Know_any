## Mermaid reserved keyword learning
- In mermaid sequenceDiagram, these are reserved and cannot be used as participant IDs (case-insensitive): `loop`, `alt`, `opt`, `par`, `critical`, `break`, `rect`, `note`, `end`
- Workaround: use different names like `MAIN`, `SCAN`, `SCHED` instead
